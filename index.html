Floor Cleaning Robot


The floor cleaning robot is a smartphone-controlled device designed to clean the floors of your house. It features rotating mops on the front and a movable foam roller at the back. The mops, made from repurposed CDs and rags, provide effective cleaning. The foam roller, similar to the ones used for painting walls, can be lifted when not in use.


To operate the robot, you can use a smartphone app that communicates with the robot via Bluetooth. The commands are sent to an Arduino UNO microcontroller connected to an HC-05 Bluetooth module, enabling convenient control of the robot's movement.


The robot is powered by a 12V lead-acid battery, which is ideal for running the motors. The driver motors have a speed control feature, allowing you to adjust the robot's movement speed. The driver motor pair operates at 100rpm, while the mops are driven by 75rpm plastic motors.


Additionally, the robot includes a water pump and a water reservoir. When necessary, you can activate the water pump to moisten the mops for a more thorough cleaning of the floor.
It's important to note that this version of the floor cleaning robot is designed for smaller areas and may not be suitable for larger spaces. While there are possibilities for adding more features, such as autonomous functionality, it was not implemented due to time constraints.

Automatic Motor Bike Stand Slider


Motorcycle side stands are a universal feature for easy parking, but they can cause accidents if left down while riding. To prevent this, a new type of side stand has been developed that automatically retracts through mechanical and electronic arrangements. A speed sensor detects wheel rotation and sends a signal to a microcontroller, which activates a DC motor to disengage the stand from the ground. The side stand can be bolted or welded to the bottom bracket of the motorcycle frame, and is held in place by an over-centered spring. Other designs retract automatically when the motorcycle is lifted or contacts the ground while moving. Various concepts have been considered to address this safety hazard.
